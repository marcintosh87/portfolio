Who Pays for Benefits

It depends. Some benefits are given to you for free. Some are split between you and Memorial. Some are offered to you at a group discount, and others are paid for by you. In the last year alone, Memorial spent $187,127,000 on employee benefits. We care about keeping employees healthy - physically, mentally, and financially, so we spend 60% of our operating budget on benefits and salaries.
