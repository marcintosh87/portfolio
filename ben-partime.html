Accrual Formulas for Your Paid Leave

You can begin using your per-pay-period paid leave hours after you’ve been employed with Memorial for six months – unless your department is closed for a holiday. In that case, you may use paid leave hours for the holiday prior to your six-month anniversary.

Part-Time Employee

You’ll accrue 0.0924 hours of paid leave for every hour you are paid. For example, if you work 64 hours per pay period, you’ll accrue 5.91 hours per pay period.
