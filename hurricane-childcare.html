Prepare Now for 2020 Hurricane Season

The 2020 Atlantic hurricane season began on Monday, June 1. We regret that due to CDC guidelines and COVID-19 social distancing restrictions, Memorial will not be able to offer emergency child care or host physician or staff families on-site during a tropical storm or hurricane. Which means that now’s the time to think about your preparations – and make plans for your children, family members, and pets – before a tropical system heads our way.

To assist you in making those plans, we have launched a special Hurricane Preparation page on Memorial’s intranet, with information and links to many resources – such as home-supply checklists and shelter locations – that will be helpful before, during, and after a storm.

As always, we need to plan ahead for ourselves and for those we love so that, if a hurricane affects South Florida this year, we can continue to care for our patients, their families, and our community.

Questions? Talk to your manager or contact the HR team at your Memorial facility.
