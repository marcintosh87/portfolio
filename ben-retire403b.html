Memorial 403(b) plan

There’s no reason not to participate in Memorial’s 403(b) plan – you’re saving for your family’s future. You choose what percentage of your salary you wish to contribute – after which the match is available to you if you’re a full- or part-time employee who works more than 40 hours per pay period.

You can invest your contributions into the Transamerica fund of your choice. All full-time employees will automatically be enrolled after 45 days of employment at a contribution rate of 5%. You can increase or decrease your contribution amount at any time.
