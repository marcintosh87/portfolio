Employee Appreciation & Recognition

Memorial is only able to fulfill its mission because of you. That’s why we believe it’s important to appreciate and recognize you when you perform outstanding work, mark an important anniversary, or go above and beyond the call of duty to serve patients and families. Here are some of the ways we salute your contribution to healing the body, mind, and spirit of those we touch.

Service Award

Memorial is honored to have long-tenured employees and we take pride in saluting those with 20, 25, 30, 35, and 40 years of service our annual Employee Recognition dinner.

Employee Recognition Gallery

Patient First Award

This award recognizes outstanding employees, physicians, and volunteers for their achievements and commitment to providing a healing environment that is safe, efficient, customer-focused, and high quality. As we strive to remain one of the safest healthcare systems in the nation, we salute our staff for always putting our patients first.

Retirement Teas

Memorial employees tend to stay with us for years – sometimes, even decades – and when they choose to retire, one of the things we like to do is send them off with a Retirement Tea. It’s a Memorial tradition, and a celebration of the service of our long term employees.
