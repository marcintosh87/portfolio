Employee Telehealth Service

Memorial has temporarily launched a free telehealth service for you and your dependents. Here’s how to use the service:

  • Call (954) 265-8400 to schedule an appointment
  • Log into MyChart from any internet-connected device before your appointment begins
  • Click on “Visits” from a computer or “Appointments” from a mobile device
  • Select your appointment and click on Test Hardware/Test Video
  • Complete eCheck-in to start your appointment

$0 Copays at Memorial Urgent Care Centers
