See a Doctor for Free Today!

You and your dependents can now see a doctor for free*, just because you are a Memorial Healthcare System employee.

Visit either of our Urgent Care Center locations or use the exclusive MHS employee telehealth service to get started, whether or not you are a MMCP or MCHP participant.

How to Access the MHS Employee Telehealth Service

  1. Call (954) 265-8400 to schedule an appointment. Mention that you are a Memorial employee or a dependent of a Memorial employee
  2. Log in to Memorial MyChart from any internet connected device before your appointment begins
    1. From a PC, click on Visits
    2. From a mobile device, click on Appointments
  3. Select your appointment and click on Test Hardware/Test Video to test
  4. Complete eCheck-in to connect to the visit

Please refer to this tip sheet if you have any questions or need help connecting.

*Copays and coinsurance are only waived at the two Memorial Urgent Care Centers listed above.

Psychiatrists Now Available

If you’re looking for a little extra support these days, we have a new service just for you.

MemorialDOCNow is now offering non-urgent virtual consultations with psychiatrists, in addition to the non-emergency medical consultations that the telehealth service already offers, which means that you can now speak with board-certified psychiatrists who can provide education and recommendations about psychiatric symptoms that you may be experiencing as a result of the coronavirus pandemic.

Virtual psychiatric consultations are available for you, your family members, and our entire community. Remember that for a limited time, Memorial is making telehealth visits available with no co-pays – whether or not you’re enrolled in Memorial Managed Care Plan or Memorial Consumer Health plan. Just remember to include the coupon code “MemorialCares.”

For a limited time, we’re offering MemorialDOCNow for free to all employees and our entire community, simply by using the coupon code “MemorialCares”.
