Medical Plans

Memorial offers two different medical plans, giving you the freedom to choose a plan that works best for you. Both plans are administered by Community Care Plan.

Have questions? Visit Community Care Plan online at ccpcares.org or call (954) 622-3499.

Memorial Managed Care Plan (MMCP)

This plan is similar to an HMO. Specific copays apply when you receive healthcare services that are covered by the plan (after meeting the annual deductible). However, the MMCP does not cover services by providers who are out of Memorial Health Network, except in an emergency or if a service can’t be offered in-network (you’ll need prior authorization before you receive care).

Memorial Consumer Health Plan (MCHP)

This plan is a consumer-driven health plan and is similar to a PPO. It includes an annual deductible and coinsurance. It has out-of-network benefits, which meansyou can receive care from providers who are out of the Memorial Health Network at a slightly higher cost to you.
