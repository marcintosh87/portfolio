Fitness

Memorial has two on-site fitness centers at Memorial Regional Hospital and Memorial Hospital West that are open to you and your family seven days a week. Each center features the latest workout equipment, such as Pilates Reformers, Stairmasters, elliptical and rowing machines, treadmills, stationary bikes, and weight-lifting machines. You can take advantage of group exercise programs to get healthy and lose weight. Certified fitness specialists are available to help you craft the wellness plan that’s right for you.

Need to bring your little one along? The fitness centers offer free babysitting services so you can dedicate your time to your workout while they take care of your child(ren).

You can pay your membership fee via easy payroll deduction if you’re a full- or part-time employee.

Wellness

Memorial offers a number of weekly classes and the Memorial Go-Getters group, to help you stay focused on your fitness goals. Our Employee Health office holds wellness fairs at all Memorial facilities throughout the year, with free blood pressure and cholesterol checks, vision screenings, BMI evaluations, and flu shots. And as our facilities are tobacco-free, we offer nicotine replacement therapy and stop-smoking programs.
