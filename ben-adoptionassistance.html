Adoption Assistance

You already perform life-changing acts of love at work by caring for our patients and their families. Let Memorial support an act of love at home, too, through our adoption assistance program. You can receive:

  • Up to $5,000 in adoption assistance for domestic or international adoptions
  • Resources for picking an adoption service
  • Checklists for choosing providers and attorneys
  • Information on how to prepare for adoption and for welcoming your new family member into your home

If you’ve completed at least one year of continuous service to Memorial in a regular, full-time position, you’re eligible for the plan. You must be employed in a full-time status at the time that you:

  • Incur your adoption expense
  • Finalize the adoption
  • Bring your new child into your home
  • Submit your request for financial assistance

Memorial will pay up to $5,000 per adoption. The lifetime benefit maximum for any Memorial employee is $10,000. Your adoption expenses will be reimbursed by Memorial directly to you after you incur them.

Once your adoption becomes final, submit the following:

  • A completed Adoption Assistance Request Form (available here )
  • Documentation of all fees and expenses (itemized receipts, canceled checks, credit card statements, and/or paid stamped invoices)
  • The final decree of adoption

Send your form and all documentation to HRServiceCenter@mhs.net. Memorial will reimburse you within 90 calendar days of the date of your final adoption decree. (The reimbursement cannot be paid to a third party, such as an agency or a court.)

For more information, contact the HR Service Center at (954) 265-5903 or email HRServiceCenter@mhs.net.
