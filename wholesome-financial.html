Memorial Employees Federal Credit Union

MEFCU has the following mobile and electronic channels available:

  • Online Banking and Mobile App – To transfer funds and pay bills. Sign up at mefcu.org
  • Free ATMs – ATMs at all Publix stores are free to members through the Presto network
  • Remote Loan Closing – If you have a pending loan you’d like to close remotely, call (954) 265-3163 to see if you can process your transaction remotely

MEFCU members can also apply to skip their April loan payments due to current financial hardships. Skips will be processed through April 20, 2020. The skipped payment will be placed in your Share Savings Account. Download the application at mefcu.org.

Feeding South Florida Food Drives

Feeding South Florida is hosting multiple drive-through food distributions throughout Broward County. Food will be distributed on a first-come, first-served basis while supplies last. To adhere to CDC guidelines, volunteers will place your food directly in your car trunk. The list is updated daily and can be found here.

Free Legal Help

If the pandemic has you thinking about updating your personal directives or other legal documents, MetLife is ready to help, with free consultation on healthcare proxies, estate planning, elder care law and more, through Friday, July 31. You, your spouse, and you dependents are eligible. Call (800) 821-6400, Monday through Friday, 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. Identify yourself as a Memorial employee and provide your employee ID number. This offer will continue to cover legal matters that extend past July 31.

Florida Power & Light

For customers experiencing COVID-19-related hardships, FPL is granting extensions on bill payments. Visit FPL to make your extension request. You can also visit FPL Help for quick links to other FPL assistance programs.
