Virtual Doctor Visits

If you can’t make it to a doctor‘s office, you can use MemorialDOCNow. You can consult online, via your phone or tablet, with a physician privately and securely – and at no cost if you participate in either MMCP or MCHP. Otherwise, it’s $59 per visit. It’s available to everyone. ‍
