Retirement benefits and pension plan

Preparing for retirement is one of the most important things you can do. Most people rely on three main sources for retirement income – employer-funded plans (like our pension or 401(a) plan), their own contributions (like our 403(b) plan and 457 plans (b), and Social Security. Together, these sources can help you replace your income so that you can have the freedom to do what you want and live the way you want after you stop working.

The longer you are with Memorial, the more of Memorial’s contributions to your retirement are yours to keep. In the 403(b) and 457(b) plans, you are fully vested once you reach five years of participation.
