Memorial Defined Benefit Pension Plan

This plan, which is paid for entirely by Memorial, is for employees who held full-time positions at Memorial for five or more years prior to November 1,2011. It’s computed by an employee’s five-highest consecutive years over the previous 10 years, multiplied by the years of service.
