Emergency Remote Work Program

Memorial recognizes that we are in a state of emergency and that the CDC is encouraging employers to consider work from home arrangements where appropriate to limit the potential spread of the virus. As a healthcare system, most of our clinical positions require the employee to be physically present for in-person contact with our patients. However, we are taking the opportunity to explore the areas in which emergency remote work would be prudent and appropriate. Employees who are selected to work remotely during this emergency are required to sign the Emergency Remote Work Agreement.
