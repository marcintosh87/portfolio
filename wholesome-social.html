Summer Child Care

We’ve partnered with the Early Learning Coalition of Broward County to bring you child care through the middle of summer. This will replace the current child care offering at the Hollywood YMCA. Here are the details:

  • The last day for child care at the YMCA is Friday, May 8
  • Children ages 0 through 13 are invited to participate.
  • You must be a Memorial employee to participate. Please fill out this application and share it with your facility’s HR team; they’ll authorize your participation.
  • You’ll be eligible for up to 90 days of child care from the date that you start using the service.
  • All children will be screened before entering; sick children will not be able to attend.
  • You will be responsible for providing all meals for your child(ren). This may include breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks.

There are a number of facilities to choose from. Some sites do not require payment, others may have a small copay. Please contact Kathleen Isaac or Raven Williams at (954) 377-2188 for full details and to find a location that meets your needs.
