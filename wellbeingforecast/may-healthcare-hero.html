Healthcare Heroes

The Weekly Wellbeing Forecast is designed by caregivers, for caregivers. We’re from various roles across the organization - working on the front lines, as support staff, as leaders, and as physicians. We’ve joined together and formed the Memorial Resilience Committee to bring you uplifting and practical information and resources you can apply to your everyday life.

We’ll be sharing new resources with you every week. We hope they serve you well, even when the crisis is over. This week, our focus is on heroes.

“A hero is an ordinary individual who finds strength to persevere and endure in spite of overwhelming obstacles.”
- Christopher Reeves

Take some time for yourself. Join Dr. Ashwin Mehta for this week’s hero meditation. Take a moment to pause and think of all of the uplifting people you have come in contact with. Every one of us can be a hero to someone at any time.
