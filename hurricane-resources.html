Prepare Now for 2020 Hurricane Season

The 2020 Atlantic hurricane season begins on Saturday, June 1. It is important we all start to prepare now to ensure we are ready in the event of a storm.

Our first responsibility is always to patients and families – even during a hurricane or tropical storm. That’s why it’s important that you take these simple steps now to get ready:

  • Make sure your contact information is correct. Log into Talent Management to make any necessary updates to your home address, home phone, and cell phone numbers
  • Elect your hurricane Team Preference by visiting your profile in Talent Management. Your leader will review all team requests to see if they can be accommodated. (Remember: Your hurricane team preference is not final. To ensure full staffing during a storm, you may be asked to change your preference if necessary.)
  • Review your personal preparation plans for your home, family, and pets. Ensure you are properly equipped with supplies and know what to do in the event of a storm.
  • Meet with your leader to review hurricane preparation. Ask to talk about topics like these during your staff meetings:
    • Your process for monitoring and communicating weather updates
    • Your department’s staffing plan
    • our department’s preparation checklist, including keeping all electronic devices fully charged for a storm watch or warning
    • Your personal preparation plans
