Buy-Up Rates

Buy-up formula (for you)

You can increase the amount of life insurance you receive through supplemental life insurance in increments of $10,000, up to $500,000.

To calculate your cost per pay period:
  1. Start with the amount of life insurance you’d like.
  2. Multiply by the rate for your age (see chart to follow).
  3. Divide by 1,000
  4. Multiply by 12.
  5. Divide by 26.

Buy-up formula Buy-up formula (for your child, up to age 26)

Memorial offers supplemental life insurance for your child(ren) until they turn 26 in increments of $2,500, up to $10,000.

To calculate your cost per pay period:
  1. Start with the amount of life insurance you’d like.
  2. Multiply by $0.11.
  3. Divide by 1,000
  4. Multiply by 12.
  5. Divide by 26.
